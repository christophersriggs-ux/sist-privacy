Should I Surf Today? — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Should I Surf Today? is designed to be simple and respectful of your privacy.

Data We Collect
We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require an account or login.
The app uses your device's location (if permitted) to identify nearby surf spots. This location data is used only in the moment to fetch local conditions and is never stored or transmitted to us.

Third-Party Services
The app fetches surf conditions from Open-Meteo, a public weather API. No personal data is sent as part of these requests. The app uses the Anthropic API to generate responses. No personal data is included in these requests.

Analytics
We do not use any analytics or tracking tools.
